Cookies

To enrich and perfect your online experience, Bookmarks.dev uses "Cookies", similar technologies and services provided by others to display personalized content, appropriate advertising and store your preferences on your computer.

A cookie is a string of information that a website stores on a visitor's computer, and that the visitor's browser provides to the website each time the visitor returns. Bookmarks.dev uses Keycloak cookies, our open source identity provider, to enable SSO services, and to enable automatic login in the browser. Bookmarks.dev visitors who do not wish to have cookies placed on their computers should set their browsers to refuse cookies before using Bookmarks.dev's websites, with the drawback that certain features of Bookmarks.dev's websites may not function properly without the aid of cookies.

By continuing to navigate our website without changing your cookie settings, you hereby acknowledge and agree to Bookmarks.dev's use of cookies.
